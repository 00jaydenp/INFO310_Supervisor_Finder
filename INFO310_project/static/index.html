Welcome to the Supervisor Finder Service

Mission

The mission of the Supervisor Finder Service is to match students with research projects curated by project supervisors
This service creates an opportunity for students to apply for postgraduate projects with ease
while also allowing supervisors to learn more about their students.

Functionality

Students will be able to view and apply for research projects that are created by supervisors in order to make the process of finding
research projects a much easier process. Project supervisors will be able to view these applications and view the applicants
that they believe are most suitable for their respective research project.
